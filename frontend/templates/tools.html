Fertilizer Quick Guide
🌱 What is NPK?

N (Nitrogen) — Leaf & stem growth · Urea (46%N)
P (Phosphorus) — Root & flower · DAP, SSP
K (Potassium) — Fruit quality & immunity · MOP

⚠️ Never Mix These Together

Urea + DAP — apply 7 days apart
Urea + SSP — don't mix directly in field
Lime + Ammonium fertilizers — never combine

✅ Best Practices

Apply base dose before sowing
Split urea into 2–3 applications
Apply when soil is moist, not waterlogged
Foliar spray only in early morning

📞 Soil Testing Near You

Visit your nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or Soil Testing Lab. Results cost ₹5–25 and give exact NPK levels for your field.
